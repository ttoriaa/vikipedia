ZE-51 Gathering: Growth Mindset

拥抱变化 Embrace the Change

在不确定时代，变化本身就是稳定项。真正的竞争力来自于持续适应、主动学习与前瞻行动。Sustainable competitiveness comes from turning constant change into a structured capability of adaptation, learning, and foresight.

Accept that change is constant

1-2

Under-fulfilment

Generally sticks to the status quo despite a need for transformation.

尽管已出现转型需求，仍主要停留在既有做法。

3

Fulfilment

Sees the opportunities and positive aspects of transformation.

能够识别并拥抱变革中的机会与积极面。

4-5

Over-fulfilment

Accepts change as a constant, anticipates the effects of VUCA and faces new challenges with foresight.

将变化视为常态，前瞻 VUCA 影响并主动应对新挑战。

从“低风险、小步快跑”的试验开始，把每次变化沉淀成经验模板。持续复盘可将一次适应升级为可复制的长期能力。

Begin with low-risk, rapid experimentation, codify each change into reusable playbooks, and use continuous reflection to transform isolated adaptations into sustainable organizational capability.
